Our Story


Established in 2022, Pizza Haven - NY Style is a pizza restaurant located at 739 Cortaro Dr, Sun City Center, Florida. Known for its authentic New York-style pizzas, WINGS, SUBS, Jamaican Beef Patties, Potato Knish, GLUTEN Free Cauliflower Crust pizza, the restaurant offers a delightful menu featuring traditional and specialty pizzas made using the finest ingredients and a blend of artisanal techniques. With a cozy ambiance and friendly staff, Pizza Haven invites you to indulge in a slice of true New York pizza artistry."
